The Best

MUSIC BAND EVER

Ben&Ben, formerly known as The Benjamins, is a folk pop/pop rock band in the Philippines. Formed in 2015, the band has since been known for their popular hits such as "Ride Home", "Leaves", "Maybe the Night", "Pagtingin", "Lifetime", "Doors", and the critically acclaimed "Kathang Isip". One of the most popular acts nationally, they are ranked as the number one most streamed Filipino artist on Spotify in 2020.They have won several awards, including multiple Awit Awards, Myx Awards, and Wish Awards.
